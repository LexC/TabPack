# Selection And Preview

## Default Selection

- [ ] Create at least two tab groups with multiple HTTP/HTTPS tabs.
- [ ] Add at least one ungrouped HTTP/HTTPS tab and one grouped unsupported tab such as `edge://settings`.
- [ ] Click Scan grouped tabs.
- [ ] Confirm every eligible grouped tab is selected by default.
- [ ] Confirm the Eligible, Selected, and Skipped counters are correct.

## Group Toggles

- [ ] Uncheck a group checkbox.
- [ ] Confirm all tabs in that group become deselected.
- [ ] Recheck the group checkbox.
- [ ] Confirm all tabs in that group become selected again.
- [ ] Select only some tabs in a group and confirm the group checkbox is indeterminate.

## Compact Numbering

- [ ] Deselect the first tab in a group with at least three tabs.
- [ ] Confirm the remaining selected tabs are previewed as `1.html`, `2.html`, etc.
- [ ] Repeat in MHTML mode and confirm selected files are previewed as `1.mhtml`, `2.mhtml`, etc.
